    Origami Instructions and Diagrams


    Step by Step diagrams are probably the most popular and easiest to follow way to show how to fold things out of paper. Trying to find good origami instructions on the Internet can be a lot of work though. To help your search I've put together a database of free origami diagrams of animals.
    Have fun !
